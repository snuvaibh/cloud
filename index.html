Made with flour,
butter & boundless love

Velvet Crumb was born from a single, tender idea — that food made with love should feel like a warm embrace. We are a cloud kitchen rooted in the belief that every dessert tells a story of patience, craft, and care.

From hand-rolling mochi at dawn to slow-churning custards until the golden hour, every recipe in our kitchen is a quiet act of devotion. We source only the finest ingredients — real butter, farm cream, single-origin vanilla — because your moment of sweetness deserves nothing less.

"A cloud kitchen built not just on recipes, but on rituals."

— The Velvet Crumb Kitchen
